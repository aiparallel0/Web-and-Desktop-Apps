Tips for Maximizing OCR Accuracy

Get the best results from any receipt OCR software:

  1. Image Quality Matters: Use well-lit, high-resolution images (minimum 1200x1600 pixels)
  2. Flatten Receipts: Smooth out wrinkles and folds before scanning
  3. Minimize Glare: Avoid reflective surfaces and harsh lighting
  4. Choose the Right Model: Use specialized models for specific receipt types (e.g., PaddleOCR for Chinese receipts)
  5. Enable Preprocessing: Let the software enhance images automatically
