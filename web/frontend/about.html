About Receipt Extractor

Transforming how businesses handle receipt data since 2024

Our Mission

At Receipt Extractor, we believe that managing receipts shouldn't be a time-consuming chore. Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to transform the way businesses and individuals handle receipt data extraction.

Founded by a team of AI researchers and software engineers, we've developed a platform that combines multiple OCR technologies with advanced machine learning models to deliver industry-leading accuracy in receipt data extraction.

What We Do

Receipt Extractor uses a proprietary AI pipeline that combines:

  • Multiple OCR Engines: Including Tesseract, EasyOCR, and PaddleOCR for robust text recognition
  • Advanced AI Models: Donut and Florence-2 for document understanding and structured data extraction
  • Smart Post-Processing: Pattern matching and validation to ensure accurate merchant names, dates, and totals
